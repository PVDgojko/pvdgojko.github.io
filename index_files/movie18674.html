Description: Following the defeat of Germany in WWII, the Allies determine that there must be an accounting of German war crimes. The preparations for the trial, the trial itself and its aftermath are shown through the eyes of Chief Prosecutor Robert Jackson and through the eyes of Reichsmarshal Hermann Goering, the ranking Nazi defendant.
